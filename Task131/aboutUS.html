KIDZ Arts & Crafts Studios

Get Creative!

Kidz Arts & Crafts offers projects for all ages: explore paint-your-own-pottery, glass fusing, paint-on-canvas, and more.  Just drop-in and do a project.

Reservations are recommended for groups of 8 or more.  Our pricing is all inclusive.  We don’t charge a studio fee--you simply pay per project.  There are many options in the range of $18 to $24.

Choose from a large selection of ceramics, including figurines, banks, bowls, mugs, plates, platters, and more, to create a lasting and usable work of art.  We carry over 65 beautiful paint colors.  Plan your one-of-a-kind design, and feel free to use our many stamps, stencils, silkscreens and sponges.  We will glaze and fire your pottery, and it will be ready in approximately 10 days.

All our paints are non-toxic, lead-free, and food-safe. Hand-wash your piece as you would any fine hand-painted pottery.  We recommend that you do not place your pottery in dishwashers and microwaves.
